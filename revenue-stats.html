About Hint Packages

Galactic Grid offers three hint packages to assist players in solving increasingly challenging puzzles. Each package is designed to provide different levels of assistance, with pricing that reflects the value provided. All purchases are processed securely through RevenueCat, ensuring a seamless experience.

Our in-app purchases are non-consumable, meaning once purchased, hints remain available forever. Revenue from hint purchases helps support ongoing development and maintenance of Galactic Grid, allowing us to continue providing new levels and features to enhance your gaming experience.
