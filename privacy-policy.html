Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection and Usage

This application collects anonymous usage data to improve the user experience. The collected data may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • User interactions within the app
  • Anonymized usage statistics
  • Purchase information (non-personally identifiable)

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII).

2. Analytics with Mixpanel

We use Mixpanel, a third-party analytics service, to track and analyze app usage. Mixpanel may collect the following data:

  • Pages visited and features interacted with
  • Time spent on different sections of the app
  • Links clicked and navigation patterns
  • Device information (browser, operating system, screen size)

This data helps us understand how users interact with our app, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall user experience. All data is collected anonymously and in aggregate form.

You can opt out of Mixpanel analytics tracking by declining cookies when prompted or by contacting us directly.

For more details, refer to Mixpanel's Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases and RevenueCat

Our app features in-app purchases for hint packages, which are processed through RevenueCat, a third-party service that manages in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect the following information:

  • Purchase history (products purchased, dates, prices)
  • App store receipts
  • Device identifiers
  • Country or region of purchase

This information is used to:

  • Process and validate your purchases
  • Provide access to purchased content and features
  • Analyze purchasing trends and patterns (in anonymized form)
  • Provide customer support for purchase-related issues

For more information about how RevenueCat handles your data, please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

All collected data is securely stored and used solely for improving the app's performance and providing purchase-related services. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties except as described in this policy or when required to process purchases and provide our services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about your purchases
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy-related concerns
